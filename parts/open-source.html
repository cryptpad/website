Open-Source

We're not interested in monopoly or locking users into our platform, so we've made our complete platform fully open-source! New releases are published and deployed to our flagship instance at least once per month. Subscribing to a premium account entitles you to priority support directly from the CryptPad team.

For those that prefer to host the platform themseves, its code is provided as-is, with no warranty, as described in its license. We offer a variety of premium options for our expert support and related services. All proceeds directly support the project's continued development.
