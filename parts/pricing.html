Benefits of having your own CryptPad instance:

  • Your own domain
  • Customizations such as logo and colors
  • Updates with new features 4 times a year
  • Fully managed instance incl. backups
  • Support for your administrators
  • Integration with existing Single Sign On schemes (coming 2022)

In most cases, personal use is best served by joining an existing public instance
