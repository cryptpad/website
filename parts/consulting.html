Maintenance

Anyone is free to use or modify the code we've written, but we have the knowledge and expertise to do so without compromizing the integrity of its privacy-preserving features. Improvements that are funded as part of our consulting packages and custom development are often integrated into the open-source project. Sponsored development of new features addresses the needs of your organization and improves the platform for everyone.
