Benefits for nonprofits

Protect volunteers and at-risk people

CryptPad is the only collaborative plateforme that really preserve your privacy. Fully open source, designed from the ground up to know as little as possible about you. We don’t have and will never access to your data.

100% collaborative software suite

Collaborate securely on your projects! We baked some really useful features like Contacts, Teams, Sharing & Access restrictions into CryptPad to help you precisely control who has access to your collaborative documents.

An open source project that matter

CryptPad is open source software, freely available. Help us make it sustainable for the years to come. Buying our services directly help us building a better project for everyone, free or paid users.

We are easy to trust

You wouldn’t trust a tech giant your documents. Google built a US$257.6 billion ads empire thanks to every user feeding them their data and metadata for free. At CryptPad we want to know as little as possible about you, and we can’t access your content.
